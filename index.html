Gorozas biografia:

Nika "Goroza" Gorozia (gori, gorchita, goroza, poroza, po, nika) daibada gaizarda da kidev unda rom gaizardos afxazetshi, mis samshobloshi, tumca amjerad uwevs gaizardos mis skamze F blokis 207 otaxshi. Mas uyvars telefonis scrollva kompiutertan da sachmlis momzadeba 8 kacistvis. Mas ar uyvars roca awuxeben da ar unda rom iyos bednieri
